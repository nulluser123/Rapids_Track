# Product Context

## Why this project exists
To provide a customized, visually appealing way to track Chess.com rapid ratings for a specific group of players, offering deeper insights than the standard Chess.com interface.

## Problems it solves
- Tracking a curated list of players.
- Analyzing performance trends, volatility, and head-to-head metrics.
- Identifying active vs inactive (MIA) players.

## UX Goals
- Emphasize a "glassmorphic" and premium dark mode aesthetic.
- Include micro-animations.
- Present data-dense dashboards in an understandable way.
- Provide a "Spotify Wrapped" style dashboard for player stats.
